About Zan Academy

Our Mission

Zan Academy is committed to providing high-quality education in the field of technology. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in the fast-paced world of tech.

Our Courses

At Zan Academy, we offer a diverse range of courses, including:

  • Python Programming
  • Advanced Diploma in Full Stack Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Science
  • Ethical Hacking

Our courses are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in the tech industry.

Certificates

Upon successful completion of our courses, students receive certificates that are recognized and respected in the tech industry. These certificates demonstrate the skills and expertise they've gained through our programs.

Internships

We also offer internship opportunities to our students, allowing them to gain real-world experience and apply their knowledge in practical settings. These internships provide a valuable stepping stone to a successful tech career.

Placement Support

Zan Academy is dedicated to helping students secure job placements in the tech industry. We provide placement support and assistance in connecting our graduates with job opportunities that match their skills and aspirations.
